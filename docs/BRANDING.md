# Branding — Kryonexus

## Logo usage
- Primary asset: `assets/branding/logo.svg`
- Recommended display size: 64px to 128px on README/doc pages
- Keep clear space: at least 20% of logo width around all sides

## Design intent
- Concept: Cryo nexus grid
- Initial mark: `K`
- Palette: `#22C55E` → `#14B8A6` gradient over deep navy base

## Export guidance
- Keep SVG as source of truth
- Generate PNG variants only for social cards or marketplaces
